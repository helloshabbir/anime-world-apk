Which anime site has no ads? +

Netflix, Funimation, and HiDive are top ad-free platforms for anime in 2025. These services offer premium subscriptions that remove all advertisements. Some free platforms like Bilibili have minimal ads compared to others.

Is Crunchyroll ad-free? +

Crunchyroll only becomes ad-free if you purchase their premium plan. The free version includes advertisements before and during episodes. The premium subscription also unlocks HD streaming and simulcast episodes.

Are there any legal free anime sites without ads? +

Truly ad-free legal anime sites typically require payment. However, some platforms like Bilibili and YouTube's official anime channels offer content with minimal ads. Many regional platforms also provide anime with fewer ads than international services.
